Our Mission


At ResearchIT, we're creating an avenue for young people to come together and tackle the world's most pressing issues. And as trite as this may sound, we're dedicated to harnessing the power of technology and community to address and solve social problems. We bring together a diverse group of intellectuals—developers, designers, writers, philosophers, creatives really, and deep thinkers—to create innovative solutions. It's a collaborative environment, and we ensure that every voice is heard and valued. So, join us @xlve. Learn, build, and own the rights to your solutions, and let's make a difference together.
