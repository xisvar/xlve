about xlve

xlve is a hub of creativity and innovation.

we're passionate about supporting creativity in all its forms. we believe great ideas can come from anyone, which is why we offer microgrants for unique, ambitious, & creative builders.

we fund passionate and driven individuals with microgrants ranging from $100 to $1000.

our goal is to empower those who want to push boundaries and make a positive impact.

xlve was founded on the idea of making resources accessible to everyone, especially aspiring innovators who may not have traditional support.

through our microgrant program, we don't just provide financial support; we create a community where people collaborate and share ideas.

by funding these projects, we aim to inspire future generations to see creativity as a way to make a difference.
