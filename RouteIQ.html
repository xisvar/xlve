Challenge

Reading content online, in form of books and podcast just sucks.

Solution

We are building a platform that allows users to read e-books, podcasts and documents with better formatting and note making functionality.

Product

RouteIQ gives you a space to store all things you want to read: books, research papers, podcast transcripts.

With AI-based formatting, you get a better reading experience. We also allow you to make notes and query the content to find specific answers using LLMs.
